Cryo-EM: Overfocus & Underfocus in TEM

How objective lens strength affects phase contrast · 300 kV · Cs = 2.7 mm

OVERFOCUSΔf = −0.5 μm
Bright fringe outside edge
  • Lens too strong — image plane above detector
  • Fresnel fringe on outside of edges
  • CTF sign reversal → contrast inversion
IN FOCUSΔf = 0
No Fresnel fringes
  • Exact focus — image plane = detector
  • Phase objects produce no contrast
  • Highest resolution but invisible specimen
UNDERFOCUSΔf = +1.0 μm
Dark fringe inside edge
  • Lens too weak — image plane below detector
  • Fresnel fringe on inside of edges
  • Standard cryo-EM: 0.5–1.5 μm underfocus
